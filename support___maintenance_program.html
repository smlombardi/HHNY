Support & Maintenance Program in New York

Once you have completed our 8-session program, you have the opportunity to reinforce the tools and behaviors you have put into place with our support program. Through this process, you will continue to be given new materials and concepts that reinforce what you have accomplished, while taking you to the next level so you feel very comfortable and at ease with you new way of thinking about food, life, and taking care of yourself. We want to ensure that you continue to approach food in a healthy manner, make better food choices, eat less, and are no longer emotionally eating.

Options include:

  • One-on-One In-Person Support Sessions with Your Counselor
  • Online Support Program ($40 per month)

3 Steps to Overcome Emotional Overeating

1 Identify Your Eating Triggers

Emotions, environment, habit, food cravings, what is sending you to the food?

2 Gain Control of Food and Your Life

Learn how to overcome the core challenges of why you have been overeating and/or making poor food choices.

3 Lose Weight Naturally

Finally approach food in a healthy manner so you can lose weight naturally.

Our Founder's Story

After 10 years of not needing food to deal with my life, maintaining my weight, exercising regularly, and approaching food in a healthy manner, I realized how fortunate I was to be free of this painful cycle that had once ruled my life. I decided if I could break out of this pattern, then others could too.
