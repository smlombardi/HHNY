Individual Weight Loss & Emotional Eating Phone or Skype Counseling & CD Program in New York

The Hungry Heart program is designed to empower you to achieve the consistent results you deserve in one of the most important areas of your life.

You will work one-one with your personal Hungry Heart counselor in the privacy of your own home or office. The 8 audio and visual session materials will guide you through the process of filling in the gaps of where you are right now and where you would like to be. This program is modeled after individuals that have already achieved real results.

Through each session, you will make slow steady changes in how you are approaching food and dealing with the underlying issues of why you've been turning to the food. We work together as a team so you can lose the self sabotage and internal struggle and gain back the quality of your life. Learn to feel better about yourself, lose the insatiable appetite for food, and come to peace with your body. Your partnership with your Hungry Heart counselor will give you the edge to finally break out of this cycle. We can't do this for you, but we can do it with you! This is a confidential, safe and discreet environment where you will get positive results.

The time to end your battle with food is now.

Program Includes:

  • 8 Session Audio Program that you listen to in the privacy of your home or office via CD and with Accompanying Session Materials (1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes of material)
  • Pre-recorded Hypnotherapy and Nutritional Counseling
  • 15-20 minute Phone or Skype appointments
  • Additional Online Tools and Resources
  • Daily Affirmations
  • Facebook Messaging for Questions and Support

3 Steps to Overcome Emotional Overeating

1 Identify Your Eating Triggers

Emotions, environment, habit, food cravings, what is sending you to the food?

2 Gain Control of Food and Your Life

Learn how to overcome the core challenges of why you have been overeating and/or making poor food choices.

3 Lose Weight Naturally

Finally approach food in a healthy manner so you can lose weight naturally.
