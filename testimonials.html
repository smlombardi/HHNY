Hungry Heart Client Testimonials on How to Lose Weight

Since 1996 The Hungry Heart program has been helping people from all walks of life to overcome over eating and weight loss issues.

The Hungry Heart started assisting clients in New York and were amazed at the results they were seeing consistently. By giving clients the tools to create permanent life style changes, and working through resistance and self sabotaging behavior, clients would feel better about themselves, take better care of themselves and lose weight naturally.

The Hungry Heart has shared this process with other counselors that have struggled and worked through their issues with food. We have put together an entire process to help others in an efficient and effective manner to get the results they are looking for. If we can do it, so can you!

Desiree's Story

        

Eating, snacking, binge eating and compulsive overeating were all synonymous to me. The means to temporarily filling up the emptiness that I felt inside. I would eat everything insight in hopes of feeling full. Yet, the feeling of fullness never came. Instead guilt, shame, disgust, and an overwhelming feeling of how I had messed up would fill my heart and mind. At night I would obsess about how I needed to fix all of the damage I had done. "Tomorrow" held many meanings; it meant over exercising, starving, starting a new diet, and ultimately knowing that I would fail again. "Tomorrow" was not a word with hope behind it. Hope had vanished when guilt and shame took over my life.

I knew that I could not become the person that I needed to be without help. The Hungry Heart team cares. With their help I learned to feel my emotions instead of eating them. I now see "tomorrow" full of opportunities and my days are full of hope. I learned to recognize patterns in my behavior which helps me to make healthier choices.

As a counselor at Hungry Heart I know how beautiful "tomorrow" is when we are emotionally and physically healthy. I look forward to working with you. Together we can stop the negative self-talk and change your internal recording to one of compassion, and hope for a healthy future.
