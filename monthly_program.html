Weight Loss Counseling & Emotional Eating - $40 Monthly Online Program in New York

The Hungry Heart Program has been created to empower you to achieve the real results you deserve. You'll work through the online program in the privacy of your own home or office, receiving access to two audio and visual session materials per month.

The 8 audio and visual session materials will guide you through the process of filling in the gaps of where you are right now and where you would like to be. This program is modeled after individuals that have already achieved real results. Through each session, you will make slow steady changes in how you are approaching food and dealing with the underlying issues of why you've been turning to the food. We work together as a team so you can lose the self sabotage and internal struggle and gain back the quality of your life. Learn to feel better about yourself, lose the insatiable appetite for food, and come to peace with your body.

Clients will automatically move into our ongoing support and maintenance process. This program can be cancelled with 10 days notice from your next month's start.

We can't do this for you, but we can do it with you!

Program Includes:

  • 8 Session Audio Program with Accompanying Session Materials (two sessions per month) that you listen to in the privacy of your home or office. (1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes of material)
  • Pre-recorded Hypnotherapy and Nutritional Counseling
  • Online Tools and Resources
  • Daily Affirmations
  • Facebook Messaging for Questions and Support

Optional Add On:

  • One-on-One Phone, Skype, or In-Person Appointments with Counselor

1 Identify Your Eating Triggers
