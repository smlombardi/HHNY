In Office Program in New York

We have created a step-by-step program that teaches clients how to stop the yo-yo dieting and lose the weight naturally. Our nutritional counselors, behavior modification specialists, and hypnotherapists will show you how to move past your negative preoccupation with eating and weight loss and move towards a more rewarding and satisfying life. During the 8-session program, clients will typically meet one-on-one in-person with their Hungry Heart counselor for an hour and 15 minutes once a week or once every two weeks over a two-to-four month period. During these sessions, clients are provided additional tools and resources to help them learn how to overcome their issues with food. After completing the program, clients have the opportunity to continue through our support and maintenance program.

Overcoming emotional overeating is a process that requires you to make taking care of yourself and meeting your personal needs priorities. It takes work to overcome emotional eating and life's challenges. Through this process, you will learn new tools and responses to deal with the stress and challenges we confront in life. Learn to enjoy eating the foods you love without gaining weight or feeling guilty and end the diet/binge cycle. We can't do it for you, but we can do it with you. This is a team effort!

Work one-on-one in-person with a Hungry Heart Counselor through our 8-session program.

3 Steps to Overcome Emotional Overeating

1 Identify Your Eating Triggers

Emotions, environment, habit, food cravings, what is sending you to the food?

2 Gain Control of Food and Your Life

Learn how to overcome the core challenges of why you have been overeating and/or making poor food choices.

3 Lose Weight Naturally

Finally approach food in a healthy manner so you can lose weight naturally.
